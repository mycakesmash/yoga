YOGA WITH EMILY

hi - im emily!

I teach yoga (asana) in the Great North Woods of beautiful New Hampshire. I can be found hiking with my chocolate lab, trail running, or catching sunrises. I began my yoga practice in 2019 in search of community and connection. I found a deep love for the ancient practice which encourages mindfulness and self-discovery.

Emily recently completed her 200-hour YTT with Fluid Yoga, an approach to movement that allows for freedom and exploration while aligning the body in a safe and efficient manner. She believes yoga is for every body shape and you do not need to be flexible or strong to begin a practice.

Emily hopes to bring students deeper into their bodies by connecting with their breath and becoming aware of their minds. Emily encourages beginners to give it a try and she strives to provide a friendly, safe and inclusive environment for everyone.
